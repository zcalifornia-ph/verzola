# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a respectful, harassment-free, and inclusive community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Use respectful and constructive language.
- Assume good intent and give actionable feedback.
- Focus discussions on technical outcomes and shared goals.
- Respect privacy and confidentiality.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, intimidation, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Sexualized language or unwelcome sexual attention.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, and project communication spaces.

## Reporting

Report violations to `zecalifornia@up.edu.ph`.
Include links, screenshots, and relevant context when possible.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may remove content, reject participation, or apply temporary/permanent bans for serious or repeated violations.
